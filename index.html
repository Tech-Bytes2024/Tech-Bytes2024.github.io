Our Approach 

Locomotion is a primal function of all living beings, from the smallest insect to the largest mammals, enabling them to navigate an ever-changing, unstructured world. What sets nature apart is its ability to bypass the need for deep, granular understanding—organisms don’t analyse their surroundings in intricate detail; instead, they perceive patterns, correlate key features, and instinctively respond to their environment.

Our technology mirrors this fundamental principle by forgoing heavy classification tasks and focusing on adaptive perception. Rather than overburdening models with exhaustive categorisation, we teach them to intuitively recognise relevant environmental patterns, allowing for fluid and efficient motion.
